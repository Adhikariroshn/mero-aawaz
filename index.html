About Us

Mero Aawaz is a citizen complaint portal with the main objective of reducing the distance between citizens and local government.

Our goals:

  • Enable citizens to easily register their problems
  • Make the problem resolution process transparent
  • Help local government understand area problems
  • Bring positive change to the community

This portal is operated with the cooperation of local government and civic organizations.

How It Works

1

Identify the Problem

Identify any problem or disorder in your area.

2

Register the Problem

Use our form to provide details, location and photos of the problem.

3

Send to Us

Submit your problem and we'll forward it to local government.

4

Wait for Resolution

Receive information that your problem is in the resolution process.

Types of Problems

Road Problems

Potholes, broken roads, road obstructions, etc.

Garbage Management

Garbage not collected, overflowing bins, etc.

Safety Issues

Street lights not working, dark alleys, crime, etc.

Water Problems

No water supply, leaking water, water pollution, etc.
